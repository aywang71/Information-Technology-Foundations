About

The Gentle Men is a band formed in 2019 by two men who have never met in person. They are composed of Charles white and Troy McKubre.

Both members of the Gentle Men maintain online presences, Charles White is known on YouTube as penguinz0 and Troy McKubre is known on YouTube as Solstate.

Charles is based out of Florida in the United States while Troy is based out of Auckland, New Zealand. All of their music is created remotely.

In April 2019, their first single "2019 Guy" was released. The song became a hit amongst the YouTube community, and was featured in PewDiePie's YouTube Rewind 2019. In 2020, the band released five more singles in February, May, June, October and December titled "Obsession", "Real to Me", "Skynut", "2004 Breakup", and "I Am Truth" respectively. Collectively, they are released in the band's first album, "The Evolution of Tears"
