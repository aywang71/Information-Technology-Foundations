North Carolina is the state that is located above South Carolina. It is oftentimes reffered to as the "better Carolina". I say that it is unfair to say so because I can guarentee most people only say it is better because the roads are better; nobody gets into the minutiae of the states and does a real comparison. I say let us enjoy North Carolina without un-needed comparisons and let us enjoy the varied landscapes such as the tall mountains and the high seas. The waves are splendid and so is the skiing. Much love North Carolina.

North Carolina has a long history as one of the original thirteen founding colonies of the United States of America. It was first discovered by Sir Walter Raleigh of England, who founded the Roanoke colony before being recalled by the Queen to fight in the Spanish-English war. North Carolina was also home to the wright brothers, who invented the airplane. This is why their license plates read "First in Flight".
